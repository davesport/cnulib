An article's title reflects its content. When you review an article's title, what does it tell you about the author's message?

Patterns of pain location in music students: A cluster analysis.

  • cluster analysis: this is a specific type of study
  • patterns of pain location: what is measured in this study
  • students: the participant group in this study

Ask yourself:

  • Are these elements relevant to my research topic?
  • Do I have other articles in my research paper citations list that are on the same topic? (Is that good, or is that a problem?)
